About Me
Heather is a wife, a mother of a son, a former belly dancer, and a lover of all things magic. She discovered yoga over 15 years ago and fell in love with it. After her first teacher training in 2013, she knew she wanted to share this knowledge with others and she started teaching right away.
What To Expect
Asana: Sanskrit term which is often translated as “posture” or “pose”
Pranayama: regulation of the breath through certain techniques and exercises
Mudra: a symbolic gesture of the hands and fingers
Meditation: to engage in mental exercise for the purpose of reaching a heightened level of spiritual awareness
Yoga is the journey of the self, through the self, to the self.
